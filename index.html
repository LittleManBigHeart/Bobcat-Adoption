Man Brings Home Kitten That Turns Out To Be a Bobcat Cub

This man thought that the bobcat cub pictured below was a kitten. You can learn more about it on youtube. He told us that when he found out, he said he was "tweaking". Officers arrested him later this week for illegal possession of methamphetamine.

Police released a statement saying that it's illegal to domesticate a wild animal.

They said that, "This man has reminded us how often the community we serve needs to be reminded".
